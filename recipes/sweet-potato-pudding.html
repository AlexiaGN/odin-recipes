Jamaican Sweet Potato Pudding


Description

This classic Jamaican dessert is moist and rich with an indulgent taste that will have your mouth watering in seconds. The combination of sweet potato and coconut makes this Caribbean dessert one you will crave for often. This recipe from Serenalissy is a modified version of what is used in Jamaica.

Ingredients

  • Half cup unsweetened desiccated coconut
  • One and a half pounds sweet potato
  • Half a pound of yam
  • 3 tablespoons of freshly grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups cocnut milk
  • A quarter cup melted unsalted butter
  • A quarter cup cornmeal
  • A third cup all-purpose flour
  • One and a half cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • A half cup golden raisins soaked in 3 tableshpoons of dark rum overnight

Topping Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • A half teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • A half tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • A half cup coconut milk
