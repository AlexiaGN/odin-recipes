Banana Bread


Description

Fluffy, moist banana bread is the perfect desert to warm you up and fill you with nostalgia. This recipe creates a delicious loaf with sweet chunks of banana all throughout.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (or 175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  4. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. This should take about 60 minutes.
  7. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
